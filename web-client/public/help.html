What game is this?

This game is an adaption of a board game called Labyrinth. The rules follow the rules of the original game, with the notable exception that all players have the same objective.

What is the objective?

Move your piece through the labyrinth and reach the objective . You can play alone, trying to find the minimum number of moves to reach the . You can also play against others and prevent them from reaching the . The pathways of the labyrinth are altered at each player's turn.

What do I have to do?

When it is your turn to play (as marked by the green box in the score board), you need to perform two actions:
  • First, rearrange the labyrinth. To do so, drag the maze cards along a row or column. Every second row and column is draggable. This action inserts the leftover maze card. You can also rotate leftover before inserting it.
  • Then move your piece, by clicking on a maze card connected to your current position. The available cards are marked with your color.
The no-pushback rule disallows reverting the previous player's shift. When the is reached, a new objective location is chosen randomly.
